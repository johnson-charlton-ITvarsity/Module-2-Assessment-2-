CRUELLA

PG-13 || 2 hr 14 min

CAST : Emma Stone; Emma Thompson; Mark Strong; Paul Walter Hauser; Joel Fry; Emily Beecham; Kirby Howell-Baptiste; John mccrea.

CRUELLA contains several sequences with flashing lights that may affect those who are susceptible to photosensitive epilepsy or have other photosensitivities. “Cruella,” which is set in 1970s London amidst the punk rock revolution, follows a young grifter named Estella, a clever and creative girl determined to make a name for herself with her designs.
