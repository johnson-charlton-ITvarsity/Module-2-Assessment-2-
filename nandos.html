NANDO'S

Nando's is a fast food chain that specialises in flame-grilled peri-peri style chicken. Founded in Johannesburg, South Africa in 1987, Nando's operates over 1,200 outlets in 35 countries. Their logo depicts the Rooster of Barcelos, one of the most common symbols of Portugal
