RUTLAND BOOKS

We are a proud second hand bookstore of endless rooms with over 60 000 books to chose from in Pretoria. Should we don't have the book you are looking for, submit it to our wanted list and we will do our best to find it for you. We sell and on a selective basis exchange & purchase second-hand books. If we can't use the books you don't want, then we redistribute them to various charities..
