CUM BOOKS

For more than 80 years the purpose of C.U.M. Books has been to put Christian content and Bibles into the hands of the people of Africa. As a publisher and retailer, the hope of the gospel remains our driving passion. Christelike Uitgewersmaatskappy (C.U.M.) was established on the eve of the Second World War when founders Freddie and Ria Crous answered a call from God in 1939 to spread the gospel through the printed word.

Through the thousands of employees who have worked tirelessly within the organisation over decades, we have touched diverse audiences with publications that reflect relevance, excellence, and integrity.
